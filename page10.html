Join the Fun & Challenging Math Contest – Supporting SickKids

      Get ready for a day filled with brain-teasing challenges and meaningful giving! On September 7th, 2024, we’re hosting a Fun & Challenging Math Contest, and we want YOU to be part of it. Whether you're a math enthusiast or just love a good challenge, this event promises a day of excitement, learning, and community spirit— all in support of SickKids!

     Registration for the contest is absolutely free, but we encourage participants to donate to SickKids, a leading hospital that provides life-saving care to children. Your generosity will help support the incredible work being done to improve the health and well-being of young patients.

     We’re thrilled to announce that thanks to your support, we’ve already raised an incredible $2,390 for SickKids! Every donation, big or small, makes a difference, and this amount will go directly to supporting the hospital’s vital services and programs.

    The contest itself is designed to challenge your problem-solving skills with a mix of fun and difficult math problems. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned math lover, there’s something for everyone. You'll have the chance to compete, learn, and, most importantly, contribute to a great cause.

     Don’t miss out on the chance to be part of this exciting event and make a real impact in the lives of children in need. Mark your calendars for September 7th, and get ready to put your math     skills to the test—all while supporting a worthy cause!

     Stay tuned for more details on how you can participate, and thank you to everyone who has already helped us raise $2,390 for SickKids!
