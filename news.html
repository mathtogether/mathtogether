MTC Members Excel at HMMT November

This month, MTC members Kaylyn and Prince joined the Leading Aces Academy (LAA) team at the renowned Harvard-MIT Mathematics November Tournament (HMMT). Held biannually, HMMT is an international epicenter for prodigious high schoolers.

HMMT consists of multiple rounds, including two individual rounds, a team round, and a fast-paced guts round that requires quick thinking and effective collaboration. They spent months preparing for this by training with past contest problems and attending weekly practice sessions. 

Competing against over 120 top teams from all over the globe, they impressively placed in the top 10 teams overall. Notably, they placed in the top 5 teams in the Guts round, and Prince placed 3rd in the overall individual rankings.
All in all, immense congratulations are due to Kaylyn and Prince for their accomplishments with LAA at HMMT. We look forward to witnessing their bright futures, and what the other members will accomplish at HMMT February!
